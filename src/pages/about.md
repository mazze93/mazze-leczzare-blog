# About Mazzeleczzare

I’m a former neuroscientist turned storyteller, marketer, and cybersecurity explorer.  
My career began in research labs mapping perception and cognition; now I map ideas and systems that connect people, technology, and imagination.

Through writing, design, and code I build bridges between technical precision and creative intuition.  
This site is my working studio—a living space for experiments in communication, brand architecture, and interactive art.

When I’m not deep in logic or language, I’m usually composing, drawing, or exploring game worlds that remind me how curiosity never retires.
